Frequently Asked Questions
How does Baitlab's AI calling work? +
Baitlab uses advanced speech synthesis and natural language processing to make and receive phone calls on your behalf. The AI understands context, handles multi-turn conversations, and integrates with your calendar and CRM to take meaningful actions during each call.
What integrations are supported? +
Baitlab natively integrates with Google Calendar, Outlook, WhatsApp, Twilio, Stripe, HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Zapier, and many more. Custom integrations are available through our REST API and webhook system.
Is my data secure and private? +
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We are SOC 2 Type II compliant and GDPR-ready. Call recordings and transcripts are stored in isolated, encrypted environments with configurable retention policies.
What are the pricing plans? +
We offer a Starter plan for individuals, a Pro plan for growing businesses with advanced automation, and an Enterprise plan with custom AI models, dedicated infrastructure, and SLA guarantees. Contact us for enterprise pricing.
